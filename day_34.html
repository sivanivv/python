Alice in Wonderland

Directed by TIM BURTON

is a live-action adaption and re-imagining of Lewis Carroll's classoc stories. The film follows 19-year-old Alice Kingsleigh, who, after a proposal at a garden party, falls down a rabbit hole and returns to the fantastical world of Underland (Wonderland). There, she reconnects with familiar characters like the Mad Hatter and learns her destiny is to end the Red Queen's reign of terror by slaying the Jabberwocky. The film blends Burton's signature dark, whimsical style with Carroll's imaginative world, creating a visually stunning and surreal adventure.






Based on a real person: The character of Alice is based on Alice Liddell, the daughter of the Dean of Christ Church College at Oxford, where Carroll taught.
